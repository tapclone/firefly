About Firefly

In the year 2002, Firefly Enterprises, an ISO 9001:2008 certified company, began its journey of finesse and quality in the Fire Fighting industry. The firefighting industry was in the nascent stage during those period and due importance was not granted to the norms and standards stipulated by various codes of the Industry. However, due to unfortunate events of occurrences of number of Fire incidents and loss of life and properties, the industry gained more importance and prominence. More Professionals were involved and that led to a drastic turnaround in the design and other parameters related to the Fire Industry Standards.
