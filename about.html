Know Us In Detail

In the year 2002, Firefly Enterprises, an ISO 9001:2008 certified company, began its journey of finesse and quality in the Fire Fighting industry. The firefighting industry was in the nascent stage during those period and due importance was not granted to the norms and standards stipulated by various codes of the Industry. However, due to unfortunate events of occurrences of number of Fire incidents and loss of life and properties, the industry gained more importance and prominence.
More Professionals were involved and that led to a drastic turnaround in the design and other parameters related to the Fire Industry Standards. FIREFLY gives full and comprehensive solutions in the field of Fire and Life Safety Industry with the ultimate aim to improve the safety and efficiency of personnel and equipment engaged in the suppression of fire through its TWO business verticals

MISSION STATEMENT

Our commitment is to ensure a quality and cost effective solution in execution processes that not only meets but would exceed our customer's expectations and set the standard for the industry.

VISION STATEMENT

Firefly Enterprises will strive to maintain its position of leadership in its industry. We will develop new methods / products to meet the demands of the fire and allied service industry.

Frequently Ask Questions

Do you provide Fire NOC?

We undertake the various Liasioning activities associated with getting the Preliminary No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the Fire Department .This will include the visits associated to the site and preparation of the initial design for the fire & safety part, Liasioning with the Fire department, inspection of the facility by the Fire Officer and to finally obtain the initial NOC from them.

Do you follow NBC/NFPA/KBR /Other related documents?

We provide cost effective design solutions keeping in mind the various statutory and Regulatory rules & obligations associated with it by following best engineering practices, adhering to various codes and standards such as NBC/NFPA/KBR /Other related documents etc. This will include preparation of the shop drawing for timely execution of the Projects
